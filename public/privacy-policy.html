Privacy Policy for TaleTunes

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address for account creation
  • Child's name and age (optional, stored securely)
  • Story listening history and preferences
  • Device information for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized story recommendations
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To send important account-related emails only
  • To analyze app performance and fix bugs

3. Data Security

We use Supabase, a secure cloud database platform, for storing all user data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

TaleTunes is designed for children aged 3-12. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • All accounts require parental email for verification
  • We collect minimal information necessary for app functionality
  • Parents can request deletion of their child's data at any time

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service providers necessary for app functionality (Supabase, payment processing)
  • When required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request account deletion at any time through the app settings.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Expo: App development and hosting
  • Google Play: App distribution

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our mobile app does not use cookies. We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or app notification.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your data:

Email: support@taletunes.app
